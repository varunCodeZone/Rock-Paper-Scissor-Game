# 🪨📄✂️ Rock Paper Scissors Game

A simple and interactive Rock-Paper-Scissors game built using **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript**. Play against the computer and track your scores with persistent storage (via `localStorage`). Responsive design included!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click on **Rock**, **Paper**, or **Scissors** to make your move.
2. The computer randomly selects its move.
3. The result is displayed via an alert popup.
4. Scores are updated and stored in your browser.
5. Click **Reset Score** to clear your progress.
